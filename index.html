About Me

Hi, my name is Eloy and I am an aspiring game developer, currently studying at Manchester Metropolitan University. I've always had an enthusiasm for gaming and am now studying to be able to expand my skill set and turn my life-long passion into my career.

I am entering my third and final year and so far have been able to work on both individual and group projects giving me experience in a variety of coding languages such as C# and C++. I have also developed other skills such as project management and game testing.

For the next step in my career, I would like to work as part of a project team and gain experience in all aspects of game development and bringing a game to life. My ultimate goal would be to project manage my own team.

I am a hard-working person with high attention to detail who will always endeavor to do my best. My drive and passion means I will always work at a high standard, whether that is individually or as part of a wider team until I am satisfied with the outcome.
