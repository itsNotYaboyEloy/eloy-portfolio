About Me

Hi, my name is Eloy and I am an project manager with a distinct liking to games from Spain, currently studying at the University of Salford. I've always had an enthusiasm for gaming and am now studying to be able to expand my skill set and turn my life-long passion into my career.

I have completed my BSc Computer Games Development course and have previous experience on both individual and group projects giving me a wide skill set of coding languages such as C# and C++.

With a successful transition into a MSc Project Management course, I hope to gain all the knowledge and skills required to becoming a reliable project manager.

For the next step in my career, I would like to work as part of a project team and gain further experience in all aspects of game development and bringing a game to life. My ultimate goal would be to project manage my own team.

I am a hard-working person with high attention to detail who will always endeavor to do my best. My drive and passion means I will always work at a high standard, whether that is individually or as part of a wider team until I am satisfied with the outcome.
